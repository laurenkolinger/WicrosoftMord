# WicrosoftMord, sample document

This is just a sample so you can see how reviewing works. **You don't have to do
anything here**, there's no obligation to comment. It's only a demo of the surface.

When you and Claude are writing for real, Claude's drafts show up in this pane as
formatted text. Whenever *you* want to flag something, you select a sentence and leave
a comment. That comment queues to Claude, who revises the text and replies, and the
change appears here live. Commenting is how you give feedback, not a step you're forced
through.

## Things you can look at

- **Citations** render as links. Coral cover has declined across the Caribbean over
  recent decades [@jackson2014], with disease and thermal stress as major drivers
  [@hughes2017]. Click a citation to jump to the reference list at the bottom.
- **Images** render inline, drop a PNG into your `docs/` folder and reference it with
  `![A reef transect](my-figure.png)`.
- **Dark mode**: the ◐ button (top right) flips white-on-black ⇄ black-on-white.
- **Font size**: ⌘+ / ⌘− (or the A+ / A− buttons) resize the document.
- **Export**: the **Export .docx** button renders this to a Word file with the citations
  linked, the format your field expects.

## Want to try the loop?

Select a sentence, click the 💬 bubble or press your comment shortcut, and leave a note. Claude revises the text, replies, and the change appears here live. This is optional.
